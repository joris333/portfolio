About Me

I finished my Civil Engineering Bachelor’s degree nominally at TU Delft in 2018. In this bachelor’s degree I incorporated a 6-month period of studying abroad in Prague, following courses about architecture. After my bachelor’s degree I started my master’s degree at TU Delft studying Building Engineering. Between September 2019 and March 2020, I worked part-time at Betonson (Van Nieuwpoort group) as a calculator of prefabricated concrete floors. Between April 2020 and June 2020, a study-related project in the Himalaya’s was prepared by three students and me. Since this got cancelled`due to the closing of the Indian borders, I am open to part-time working opportunities.

In September 2020, I am planning to start my master’s thesis. My interests are:
  • Parametric Design. This was my favourite course in my master’s degree, since it combines two things that intrigue me: Structural Design and Programming.
  • Circular Economy. I consider this to be a very interesting topic, since it is a concept which will help reduce the footprint by the Construction Industry significantly.
  • Modular building & demountability of structures.
