Multidisciplinary Project: Resilient Himalayan Homes

Since the start of 2019, three students and I have been working to create a multidisciplinary project abroad. The project concerns the re-implementation of the vernacular buildings in the Himalayan region, for future uses.

This experience taught me how to set up a complete project from scratch, and how to reach out for the right professors and information to create our project proposal.

This project was planned to happen between April and June 2020. But due to COVID-19 it is postponed.
